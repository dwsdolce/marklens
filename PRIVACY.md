# Marklens Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-02_

Marklens is a native markdown viewer for macOS and iPadOS. It runs entirely
on your device.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** Marklens does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal
data, usage analytics, crash reports, identifiers, or file contents.

## What the app accesses

- **Markdown files you explicitly open** — read locally on your device to
  render. Marklens never uploads them anywhere.
- **No network access** is performed by the application code. Marklens
  declares the `com.apple.security.network.client` entitlement only because
  Apple's `WKWebView` framework requires it to initialize its rendering
  process, even for purely local content.
- **No third-party SDKs, trackers, or analytics** are included.

## Children's privacy

Marklens does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including
children under 13.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at
